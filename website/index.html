How it works

From rough draft to ready to send.

No copying between apps. No invisible edits. You stay in control from selection to replacement.

01

Select your text

Highlight a sentence or paragraph in a supported Mac app such as Notes, Mail, Teams, or Slack.

02

Press ⌃⌥Space

Polishly opens beside the selection. Choose Improve, Concise, Friendly, Expand, or write your own instruction.

03

Review and accept

See a word-level diff, then replace the original in place or copy the result to your clipboard.

Privacy by architecture

Your text goes where you choose. Nowhere else.

Polishly does not run a rewriting backend. When you invoke a rewrite, the selected text travels from your Mac directly to the provider configured in Settings.

  • Nothing is read or sent until you press the hotkey.
  • Your API key is stored in macOS Keychain.
  • Demo mode keeps the full workflow on your Mac.
  • The source is public and MIT licensed.

Install and start

  1. 1Drag Polishly to Applications.
  2. 2Open it and allow Accessibility.
  3. 3Choose a provider in Settings, or use Demo mode.
  4. 4Select text and press ⌃⌥Space.

Need a free provider key? Create a Groq key or read the setup guide.

Polishly reads the selection only after you press its hotkey. The text then goes directly from your Mac to the provider configured in Settings. Polishly has no cloud service in the middle.

Polishly works with Mac apps whose text fields expose selections through the macOS Accessibility API, including common workflows in Notes, Mail, Teams, and Slack. Some custom editors may not expose their selection or allow in-place replacement.
