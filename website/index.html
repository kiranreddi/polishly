After you install

  1. Open the DMG and drag Polishly to Applications.
  2. Launch it from Applications (menu bar only — no Dock icon).
  3. Allow Accessibility when macOS asks.
  4. In Settings, pick Groq or Cerebras, paste a free API key, Save.
  5. Select text → press ⌃⌥Space → Accept.

Truly free AI path: create a Groq key at console.groq.com/keys (no credit card). Full steps are in the README.

Prefer source? Clone the repo and run ./scripts/package-release.sh — or open Issues on GitHub.

How it works

Three steps. No app switching.

Polishly reads your selection through macOS Accessibility, sends it only when you ask, and writes the result straight back into the field you were already in.

1

Select text anywhere

Mail, Notes, Slack, Teams, your browser — anywhere macOS can read a text selection.

2

Press ⌃⌥Space

A small card appears anchored to your selection with a streaming rewrite. Pick a tone tab, or type a free-form instruction with Revise with AI.

3

Review, then Accept

A real word-level diff shows exactly what changed. Accept replaces the text in place; Copy leaves it on your clipboard instead.
